Who We Are

Noduco was started in 2011 by a team of IT professionals from highly accredited academic and industry backgrounds. Our approach is to take ownership for problems, develop a long term strategic partnership with our clients, and work towards solving “ADM” (Application Development and Maintenance) needs. Testimonials from our clients (various levels) say that we have been able to uphold the values that we had started the company with. We have experience in developing software solutions including but not limited to CRM (Customer Relationship Management), E-Commerce, custom solutions, self service portals and integration with 3rd party vendors. We strive to be an all purpose software development and support company, serving client's custom software development needs.

Our Values

  • Complete client satisfaction is always top priority.
  • Deliver quality solutions, with speed, always relying on holistic analysis.
  • Be highly responsive and available to our clients.
  • Serve the complete needs of client – design, development or legacy support.
